About Us

"Gooning" is a term increasingly encountered in online discussions of human sexuality. It refers to a practice of deliberate sexual stimulation—typically involving repeated "edging" or bringing oneself to the brink of orgasm, then stopping before climax. This cycle is repeated over extended periods.

Although often discussed informally or humorously online, the underlying neurochemical and psychological aspects of edging behaviors are of growing interest in the fields of sexology, neuroscience, and behavioral psychology. Some individuals report entering what is described as a "pleasure trance" — a state of altered awareness or hyper-focus linked to intense arousal and prolonged stimulation.

The Gooner Organization is committed to the objective study and documentation of such phenomena in a medical, academic, and culturally sensitive context. We believe understanding these behaviors can lead to more informed discussions about pleasure, sexual health, compulsive behaviors, and mindfulness.
